INSTRUCTONS!
Click on the Start button to start the game.
Click on the screen to set up defence blocks.
Once all the blocks are setup(6) click on the screen once to initialise game.
Click on the go button to let the zombies come.
Direct your mouse and press space to shoot bullets only one at a time.
Kill the zombies.
Escape from them or else you might loose your life and if all health is lost you loose the game.
All the Best! Start Playing
